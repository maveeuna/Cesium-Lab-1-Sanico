Roblox

The game allows users to “Imagine, socialize, chat, play, create, interact and relate with others in many ways.” The Roblox Suite allows gamers to create their own game or create another world with friends or 'virtual explorers'. It's a free platform where players can dip in and out of different virtual worlds and play any game in them. It’s packed with user-created content spanning a dizzying variety of genres and titles.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • Spend time wind my friends online.
  • Improve my creativity by creating games.
  • Socialize with people around the world.
  • A way to enjoy my free time.
  • This game makes me feel calm.
